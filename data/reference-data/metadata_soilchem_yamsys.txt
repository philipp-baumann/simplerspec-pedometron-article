<sample_ID>:      Consists of
			  <country abbreviation>_<site abbreviation>_<field_no>
				<country abbreviation>:
					"BF": Burkina Faso;
					"CI": Côte d'Ivoire
				<site abbreviation>:
					"lo": Léo (Burkina Faso);
					"mo": Midebdo (Burkina Faso)
					"tb": Tiénningboué (Côte d'Ivoire);
					"sb": Liliyo (Côte d'Ivoire)
						(previously named Soubré)
				<field_no>:
					number from 01 to 20;
					separate numbering at each of the YAMSYS sites
<country>: 		Consists of <country abbreviation>:
					"BF": Burkina Faso; "CI": Côte d'Ivoire
<site>: 		Consists of <location abbreviation>:
					"lo": Léo (Burkina Faso);
					"mo": Midebdo (Burkina Faso)
					"tb": Tiénningboué (Côte d'Ivoire);
					"sb": Liliyo (Côte d'Ivoire)
<S>: 			Total sulfur in mg per kg dry soil; measured by elemental analyzer (vario PYRO cube)
					coupled to a mass spectrometer (IsoPrime100, Isoprime Ltd., UK)
<C>: 			Total carbon in g per kg dry soil; measured by elemental analyzer (vario PYRO cube)
					coupled to a mass spectrometer (IsoPrime100, Isoprime Ltd., UK)
<N>: 			Total nitrogen in g per kg dry soil; measured by elemental analyzer (vario PYRO cube)
					coupled to a mass spectrometer (IsoPrime100, Isoprime Ltd., UK)
<ex_Ca>			Exchangeable calcium in mg Ca per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_Mg>			Exchangeable magnesium in mg Mg per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_K>			Exchangeable potassium in mg K per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_Al>			Exchangeable aluminium in mg Al per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_Na>			Exchangeable sodium in mg Na per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_Fe>			Exchangeable iron in mg Fe per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<ex_Mn>			Exchangeable manganese in mg Mn per kg dry soil;
					measured by 0.1M BaCl2 extraction (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
					and ICP-OES analysis (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<pH_BaCl2>		pH measured in 0.1M BaCl2 extract (Hendershot and Duquette, 1986)
<CEC_eff>		Effective Cation Exchange Capacity in cmol(+) per kg dry soil;
					calculated from values of exchangeable cations Ca, Mg, K, Na, and Al
<BS_eff>		Base saturation in percent;
				calculated from ratio of the sum of basic cations
					(Ca2+, Mg2+, K+, and Na+) in cmol(+) per kg dry soil tot the <CEC_eff>
					multiplied by 100
<pH>			pH measured in water using a 1:2 weight-to-volume ratio of
					soil:water;
					10 g of soil; 20 mL of water
<P_resin>		Resin-extractable P in mg P per kg dry soil
					(Kouno et al., 1998);
					determination of P by malachite green method (Ohno and Zibilske 1991)
<Fe_tot>		Total iron in g Fe per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Si_tot>		Total silicium in g Si per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Al_tot>		Total aluminium in g Al per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<P_tot>			Total phosphorus in mg P per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<S_tot>			Total sulfur in mg S per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<K_tot>			Total potassium in g K per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Ca_tot>		Total calcium in g Ca per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Mn_tot>		Total manganese in g Mn per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Zn_tot>		Total zink in mg Zn per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Cu_tot>		Total copper in mg Cu per kg dry soil;
					measured by energy dispersive X-ray fluorescence (ED-XRF);
					XEPHOS instrument (SPECTRO Analytical Instruments GmbH, Germany)
<Zn_DTPA>		DTPA extractable zink in mg Zn per kg dry soil;
					determined by DTPA soil test protocol developed
					by Lindsay and Norvell (1978).
					0.0005M diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid (DTPA), 0.01M CaCl2, and 0.1 M triethanolamine (TEA).
					Element concentrations in the extract were
					determined by ICP-OES (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<Cu_DTPA>		DTPA extractable copper in mg Cu per kg dry soil;
					determined by DTPA soil test protocol developed
					by Lindsay and Norvell (1978).
					0.0005M diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid (DTPA), 0.01M CaCl2, and 0.1 M triethanolamine (TEA).
					Element concentrations in the extract were
					determined by ICP-OES (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<Fe_DTPA>		DTPA extractable iron in mg Fe per kg dry soil;
					determined by DTPA soil test protocol developed
					by Lindsay and Norvell (1978).
					0.0005M diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid (DTPA),
					0.01M CaCl2, and 0.1 M triethanolamine (TEA).
					Element concentrations in the extract were
					determined by ICP-OES (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<Mn_DTPA>		DTPA extractable manganese in mg Mn per kg dry soil;
					determined by DTPA soil test protocol developed
					by Lindsay and Norvell (1978).
					0.0005M diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid (DTPA),
					0.01M CaCl2, and 0.1 M triethanolamine (TEA).
					Element concentrations in the extract were
					determined by ICP-OES (Shimandzu Plasma Atomic Emission Spectrometer ICPE-9820)
<sand>			Percent sand determined by particle size analysis using
					the hydrometer method described in Bouyoucos (1951).
					Soil was stirred with sodium hexametaphosphate and
					deionized water.
<silt>			Percent silt determined by particle size analysis using
					the hydrometer method described in Bouyoucos (1951).
					Soil was stirred with sodium hexametaphosphate and
					deionized water.
<clay>			Percent clay determined by particle size analysis using
					the hydrometer method described in Bouyoucos (1951).
					Soil was stirred with sodium hexametaphosphate and
					deionized water.
